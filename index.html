About


This is a Bootstrap News Feed site that compiles the top stories from ESPN, FOX Sports, and BBC Sports live. Below are cards that will redirect you to their respective sites to read more. For this project I used a bootstrap page from the MDL website and followed the Material Design guidelines from Google's webpage. This allowed me to make some cards for the data. What I did was make calls to the News API from newsapi.org to get all the articles and followed by styling the rest of the site. The button in the bottom right corner will take you to my personal website.
